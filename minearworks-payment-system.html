Follow these steps in order and your full payment + delivery system will be live and processing real orders within a day. Every tool recommended is either free or low-cost.

01
Create your Stripe account
Go to stripe.com and sign up for free. Enter your bank account details so payments go directly to you. Takes 10 minutes. Once set up, go to Products → Payment Links and create one link per product. Copy each link and paste it into the corresponding Buy button href on your landing page.
stripe.com → Products → Payment Links Free to set up 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction
02
Set up Gumroad for digital delivery
Go to gumroad.com and create a free account. Upload your PDF files for each book and digital product. Gumroad gives you a product link — paste this as your Buy button URL. After purchase, Gumroad automatically emails the buyer a secure download link. No code required, no backend, completely hands-off delivery.
gumroad.com → New Product → Upload PDF Free tier available 10% fee on free plan, $10/mo for 3% fee
03
Connect PayPal to your checkout
Create a PayPal Business account at paypal.com/business. Go to Tools → PayPal Buttons to generate Buy Now buttons for each product. Alternatively, Stripe supports PayPal natively — enable it in your Stripe dashboard under Settings → Payment Methods with one click.
Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Payment Methods → PayPal One-click in Stripe
04
Enable Apple Pay & Google Pay
Both are already built into Stripe Payment Links — no setup needed. Customers on iPhone/Mac automatically see Apple Pay; Android users see Google Pay. To enable on your own checkout form, add the Stripe.js library and use the PaymentRequestButton element. Stripe's dashboard shows one toggle to enable both simultaneously.
Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Payment Methods → Wallets Already enabled in Payment Links
05
Deploy MinearWorks.com on Netlify
Go to netlify.com and drag-and-drop your HTML files into the deploy box. Your site is live instantly on a Netlify subdomain. Then go to Site Settings → Domain Management and click Add Custom Domain. Enter minearworks.com. Netlify gives you the DNS records to add in your domain registrar (wherever you bought the domain). Takes about 10 minutes; propagates in under an hour.
app.netlify.com → Add New Site → Deploy Manually Free hosting HTTPS auto-configured
06
Set up [email protected] email
Go to Google Workspace (workspace.google.com) and create a Business Starter account for ~$6/month. This gives you [email protected], a full Gmail inbox, Google Drive, and Calendar. Point your domain's MX records to Google (they give you exact copy-paste instructions). All invoices, receipts, and delivery emails will come from your professional address.
workspace.google.com → Business Starter ~$6/month
07
Set up Calendly for consulting bookings
Go to calendly.com and create a free account. Set up a 1-hour and 5-hour event type with your availability. Connect it to your Google Calendar. Add your Calendly link to the consulting section of your site. After a client pays your invoice via Stripe, send them the Calendly link to book their session automatically — no back-and-forth emails required.
calendly.com → New Event Type → 1 Hour Free plan available
08
Set up email marketing with ConvertKit
Go to convertkit.com and sign up free (up to 1,000 subscribers). Replace the newsletter form on your landing page with your ConvertKit embed code. Every subscriber goes into your list automatically. Use ConvertKit to send new book announcements, free chapters, and early access offers. When you launch a course, email your list first — they already trust you.
convertkit.com → Forms → Embed Free up to 1,000 subscribers
09
Add analytics to track sales & traffic
Create a free Google Analytics 4 account at analytics.google.com. Copy the tracking script tag and paste it just before the </head> tag on every page. Within 24 hours you'll see exactly where visitors come from, which pages they visit, and which products get the most clicks. Stripe's dashboard handles all revenue tracking separately.
analytics.google.com → Create Property → Copy script tag Completely free
10
Use this invoice system for consulting clients
Use the Invoice Generator tab in this file to create professional invoices for every consulting client. Fill in the details, click Generate, then email the invoice directly. The client clicks the pay button which links to your Stripe payment link. When paid, mark it as paid in your records. Save each invoice as a PDF via your browser's Print → Save as PDF function for your records.
This file → Invoice Generator tab → Generate → Email Already built No extra cost
