The Man Behind MinearWorks

Minear — 25 Years in the Field

MinearWorks wasn't built from a library. It was built from 25 years inside two of the most demanding trades in the industry — operating boilers, managing mechanical systems, pulling wire, reading plans that made no sense, and fighting for change orders that were always justified.

The books exist because the knowledge deserves to be written down — not sanitized into a textbook, but told honestly, with the humor and hard lessons intact.

  • Licensed Stationary Engineer — 25 years in the field
  • Boilers, HVAC, mechanical systems & electrical trades
  • Entry-level to Chief Engineer career experience
  • Published author — live on Amazon
  • Second book currently in development
